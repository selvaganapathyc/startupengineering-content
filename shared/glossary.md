# Glossary

Canonical definitions of terms used across the repo. Placeholder — terms will
be added as they accumulate.

- **Topic** — A folder containing an `index.md` (principle) and a
  `variants.yaml` (one entry per variant applying the principle).
- **Variant** — A specific way to apply a topic's principle. Authored by Selva,
  an individual, or a company.
- **Layer** — A form in which a variant is consumable: `skill` (markdown),
  `visual` (interactive component), `plugin` (agent plugin), `mcp` (MCP server).
- **Phase** — One of the six lifecycle phases: `define`, `discover`, `design`,
  `develop`, `validate`, `operate`.
- **Sub-area** — An intermediate folder under a phase that groups related topics.
  Today only `design/` has sub-areas.
