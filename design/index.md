Design is the phase where decisions become expensive to reverse. APIs,
schemas, and architecture shape the next six months. The skills here are
about making those decisions deliberately, not by accident.

Design is organized into sub-areas: `system-architecture`, `data-design`,
`api-design`, and `observability-design`. Each sub-area has its own
`index.md` describing its focus.
