---
title: System architecture
slug: system-architecture
parent_phase: design
short_description: Service boundaries, ADRs, and the front-end's relationship to the back-end.
---

Decisions about how the system is composed — what's a service, what's a
library, how components talk, what's documented where. Sub-area includes
documentation practices (ADRs) and frontend engineering, since frontend
architecture is typically where most systems' coupling becomes visible.
